The Wallet

A simple wallet.

How works?
Type text with the expense or gain value and the portfolio will automatically make your swing.

Accounts and Tags
To add a tag use the # and to add an account and use the @. Ex.: #MyTag @MyAccount

Gains
Use the + in front of the number. Ex.: +10.00

Expenses
Just type the number or place the - in front. Ex.: 12.00
